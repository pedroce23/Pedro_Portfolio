Diesel StyleLab

  1. Home
  2. Diesel

Project Description

Diesel wants to expand into higher-end casual wear with a new brand- StyleLab. Should Diesel make StyleLab an independent brand, or should there be some kind of link between the brands? What should StyleLab's logo be and who should it target?.

Recommendations

  • Diesel should use strong endorsment subranding to link its positive associations (durable clothing, irreverant image) with StyleLab, but maintain brand separation to avoid dilution
  • StyleLab's logo should say "StyleLab by Diesel" with the StyleLab part emphasized
  • StyleLab should target previous Diesel consumers who have grown too old for Diesel's clothing and whose tastes have matured
