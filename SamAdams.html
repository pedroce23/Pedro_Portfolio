Project Description

Samuel Adams is in the light beer industry with its LightShip beer. The beer did well when first introduced to market, but sales have stagnated. Should Samuel Adams rebrand LightShip? If so, how? Or should they leave the light beer industry entirely and focus their efforts on the growing craft beer segment? Taste tests say that LightShip is better than Amstel Light, but are the different associates that stem from a light beer versus a craft beer too significant for Samuel Adams to overcome?

Recommendations

  • Beer industry stagnant as a whole, but light beer market growing, thus stay in the segment
  • Rebrand LightShip to Samuel Adams' Light, with bottle label depicting Adams carrying a lantern
  • Expand light beer product line by creating various flavors of light beer, similar to craft beer offerings
  • Leverage this Craft and Light Beer partnership to control two taps at restaurants and bars
