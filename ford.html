Project Description

The Ford Fiesta is preparing to launch and Ford is allowing popular social media "agents" to participate in an organic marketing campaign. How closely should Ford manage and control the content that agents are producing? Should low-performing agents be cut and their cars be used for test-drives? How should companies manage grassroot marketing campaigns, especially with younger demographics more keen to judging ad authenticity?

Recommendations

  • Ford should high-performing agents to create their own video themes and trust their social media expertise will lead to high-quality videos
  • Ford should not mandate that agents show more of the car in videos in order to mantain campaign authenticity
  • No agents should lose their car because they are all influential and negative experiences can be brought under the public microscope
  • If a company is confident in the quality of its products, it should trust users to engage with the product any way they please
